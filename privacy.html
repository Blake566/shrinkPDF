Privacy Policy

ShrinkPDF for macOS

Effective date: March 22, 2026

ShrinkPDF is designed to compress PDF files locally on your Mac. We keep data collection to a minimum.

Information We Collect

ShrinkPDF does not require account creation and does not collect personal information through the app itself.

Files You Select

ShrinkPDF accesses only the PDF files and output folder you explicitly select in the app. These files are used only to perform compression on your device.

How Your Data Is Used

  • To open PDF files you select
  • To compress PDF content on your Mac
  • To save output files to the folder you choose

Data Sharing

ShrinkPDF does not sell, rent, or share your files with third parties.

Data Storage

PDF processing is performed locally on your device. Your selected files are not uploaded by ShrinkPDF as part of normal app use.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact apblk2026@gmail.com.
